Welcome to the world of Raspberry pi and IOT

Raspberry pi is a low-cost, credit-card size single-board computer developed in United Kingdom by Raspberry Pi Foundation inorder to promote education on a large scale at low cost. It is capable of doing desktop task such as word, spreadsheet, excel, internet browsing, gaming, programming etc. It is one of the best option for building Internet of Things and Home Automation projects currently.
